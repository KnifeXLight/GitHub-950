Anime and Manga

Background

Growing up, I never really had hobbies or interests I can call my own. I would often change my hobbies and interests growing up, joining multiple clubs and events, trying to see which one piques my interest. I participated in sporting events such as swimming, tennis, and Taekwondo. I attended arts and fine arts class including band classes, music classes and painting classes. Growing up, I played violin professionally and ended up with RCM level 10 at the end. At school, I attended academic clubs and writing clubs; I was especially interested in poetry club as it allowed me to express myself in a writing.

However, trying all these interests out, I found myself still unable to call something my own. While in my third year of university, I had a sudden epiphany about myself. I reflected upon my entire life and found a common interest while growing up. I realized that I watched Anime and read Manga growing up. I never thought watching and reading was a hobby and was just an activity to pass time. I was also told growing up that watching Anime and reading Manga was childish and was a phase I will grow out of. This was a wrong way of thinking.

I left my comfort zone and joined the Anime Club at my university. This club was inviting, diverse, and was also where I met a few of my closest friends. In this club I learned about the different paths of the Anime Fandom such as reviewers, online gamers, boardgamers, writers, and artists. I explored more into the different paths and found myself finding interest in two of the paths; the reviewing path and cosplaying path.

Cosplaying

Cosplaying is a combination of two words, costume and playing. Cosplayers are the people who participate in cosplaying; do note that cosplayers are artists. Cosplayers will design, create, and modify the COSTUMES of fantasy characters. Then in photoshoots or conventions, the cosplayers will dress up in the costume and PLAY as the fantasy character by acting as the character. Cosplaying combines the skills of design, creation, adapting, and acting.

As for me, I was first introduced to the world of cosplaying by my friend Camille. She is a professional cosplayer who has over 10 years of experience cosplaying. She introduced me to her network of friends and cosplayers where I learned tips and tricks of designing costumes and also acting. As part of the acting lessons, I learned from my friends on how to develop the body shape for the cosplay; which type of workout will train which muscle group. So, I could include gymming as a interest.
